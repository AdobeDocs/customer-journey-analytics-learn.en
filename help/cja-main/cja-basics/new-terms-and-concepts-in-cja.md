# Analytics to CJA - New terms and how they map

Learn how to translate your existing Adobe Analytics knowledge into Customer Journey Analytics by mapping familiar concepts like core metrics, report suites, variables and more to CJA equivalents. This high-level video explains the CJA relationship with Experience Platform, and shows where to find these mapped terms in the interface.
