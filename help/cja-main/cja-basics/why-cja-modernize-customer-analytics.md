# Why CJA? A strategic review of CJA vs. Adobe Analytics

Discover how Adobe Customer Journey Analytics (CJA), built on Adobe Experience Platform, overcomes the limitations of traditional Adobe Analytics. CJA delivers cross-channel and person-level insights, flexible data management, privacy-safe handling, and embedded generative AI for predictive analytics.

For more information, review [key considerations before migrating to CJA](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/perspectives/cja-readiness-three-key-considerations-before-migrating-to-cja#){target="_blank"}.
